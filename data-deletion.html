EZ Sesh Data Deletion

EZ Sesh respects your privacy and your right to control your personal data. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

How to Delete Your Data

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

  1. Open the EZ Sesh app
  2. Go to Profile tab
  3. Tap Privacy
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm your deletion request

Method 2: Email Request

Send an email to ezseshbooking@gmail.com with:

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Your registered email address
  • Your full name as it appears in the app

What Gets Deleted

  • Your profile information (name, photo, contact details)
  • Your booking history
  • Your messages and chat history
  • Your reviews and ratings
  • Your instrument and availability settings
  • Your location data

Processing Time

Your data will be deleted within 30 days of your request. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Note: Some data may be retained for legal compliance purposes (e.g., transaction records for tax purposes) for up to 7 years as required by Philippine law.

Questions?

If you have any questions about data deletion, contact us at ezseshbooking@gmail.com
